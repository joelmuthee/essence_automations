Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services.

What makes your websites "Ultra-modern"?

We combine stunning visual aesthetics like transparent glassmorphism with a focus on real results: high-converting lead generation and clickable contacts. Your site will be lightning-fast, mobile-responsive, and explicitly optimized for both traditional SEO and modern AI Search discovery.

What if I already have a website?

No problem! If you're happy with your existing website, we can simply integrate our automation tools (like the AI Chat, Review System, Booking Calendar, and Forms) into it.

For example, we integrated an AI Chat, School Visit Calendar, and Careers Form into St. Christopher's International School's existing website. You can see it in action here.

How does the Google Reviews system work?

Our system helps you build trust on autopilot. It uses Smart Filtering to protect your reputation: happy customers are guided to leave a public review on Google, while critical feedback is kept internal, so you can resolve issues privately.

How does the AI Chat work?

Our AI Chat is trained on your business info. It sits on your website 24/7, answering customer questions, capturing leads, and even booking appointments directly into your calendar. All without you lifting a finger.

You can see it in action on St. Christopher's International School's website.

How does the Online Appointment Calendar work?

Our calendar automates the entire scheduling process. You can easily Embed & Share it anywhere, and it sends automated appointment reminders to drastically reduce no-shows.

For example, we integrated this system into St. Christopher's International School's website to streamline their admissions process.

I've never run ads before. Can I still use the AI Ads Manager?

Absolutely! Our system is designed for beginners. With our easy-to-use interface and proven templates, you can launch high-converting campaigns in just 15 minutes. No prior experience needed.

Is this suitable for my industry?

Yes! Our system is built for all kinds of businesses, whether you sell products or services. We've partnered with diverse niches, from international schools and engineering firms to hardware stores. The best way to see the fit is to book a free appointment so we can explore your specific needs.

What happens if I cancel?

You can cancel at any time. You will lose access to the CRM and automation features, but you can export your contacts. We believe in earning your business every month, so there are no long-term lock-in contracts.

How fast can I see results?

We move fast. A new website takes about 3-5 days. Our Automation systems (like Google Reviews) can be live in under 48 hours. You could start getting more leads as soon as this week.

Do you provide training on how to use the system?

Yes, absolutely. We don't just hand you the keys and leave. We provide comprehensive video tutorials and 1-on-1 onboarding sessions to ensure you and your team are comfortable. Plus, we're always just a message away if you get stuck.

Is my customer data secure?

Security is our top priority. We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to keep your data and your customers' data safe. You retain full ownership of all your data at all times.

Can I upgrade my package as my business grows?

Definitely. Our solutions are built to scale. You can start with the essentials and add more advanced features (like AI Ads Manager or Workflows) as your revenue increases. We grow with you.

How does the Google Business Profile (GBP) Booster help my Local SEO?

Our GBP Booster optimizes your profile with keyword-rich updates, automated review management, and precise map pinning. This signals to Google that your business is active and authoritative, helping you rank higher in local search results and the "Map Pack".

Can you build custom automations for my unique business needs?

Absolutely. Whether you need a complex client onboarding sequence, automated contract signing, or a custom lead nurturing pipeline, we can build a tailored workflow that fits your unique business operations perfectly.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes! We work with clients globally. We accept payments via PayPal and direct bank transfers to make the process seamless regardless of where you are located.
