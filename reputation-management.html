Rajshyn Jewellers

We implemented a Smart Review System featuring a **visually stunning, interactive star-rating interface**. The premium gold aesthetics match Rajshyn's brand, making the act of rating feel inviting rather than a chore.

This "smart filter" helps manage Rajshyn's online reputation effectively:

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5 Stars): The customer is instantly thanked and presented with buttons for all three branches. They simply choose the location they visited, and the system takes them directly to that specific Google Business Profile to leave their 5-star review.

⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4 Stars): The system captures this as "almost perfect" but keeps it private. It prompts the customer to share specifically what we could have done better to earn that 5th star next time, giving you valuable insights for improvement without publicly displaying a non-perfect score.

⭐ to ⭐⭐⭐ (1-3 Stars): The system intercepts these negative experiences. Instead of directing them to Google, it opens a private feedback form. This allows the customer to vent their frustration directly to the owner via email, giving a chance to resolve the issue internally without it damaging Rajshyn's public reputation.
