Who We Are

Company Profile

Chumatech Company Ltd was started in 1996. We have grown from working with petroleum companies in Terminals, Depots, and Aviation sites to covering major installations across Kenya, Tanzania, and Uganda.

Our scope of work expanded significantly after 2013 to cover Nairobi Terminal, JKIA, Wilson, and Moi Airbase. We are guided by strict purchase contract documents setting out clear parameters including pricing, KPI performance, and HSSE guidelines.

Through programmed maintenance schedules and trained teams, we meet required levels of maintenance competency, earning green banding levels in our operations and NCA certification.
